I believe in Youtube University

Instilled in me by a high school radio broadcast teacher, I have always held the belief that anything can be self taught with the right resources. Whether it's plant care, motion graphics, or a working teddy bear animatronic, any idea can come to pass if you have the determination to experiment and dig through the internet for answers and explanations.

It is this idea that draws me to advertising, and makes being a creative so much fun. The enrichment and joy that comes from problem solving a tough execution drives me each day, and I have yet to discover an idea that couldn't be MacGyvered to life.

I have taken this attitude to organizations such as C-SPAN, Qolsys, and the BYU Marriott School of Business, and hope to take it to a New York City production house or ad agency one day. Wherever I go I'll be excited to swing at every pitch I get and am eager to see what I am capable of.
